T\+C\+P/\+IP or U\+D\+P/\+Datagram socket. This class encapsulates a T\+C\+P/\+IP or U\+D\+P/\+Datagram socket. A\+F\+\_\+\+I\+N\+ET connections following the I\+Pv4 Internet protocol are supported.
